The Name

زور Zoor

The Meaning Behind the Name

Zoor (زور) means strength in Urdu and Farsi. We named our brand after this word because we believe beard care should be powerful, natural and purposeful.

A well-kept beard is a statement. It speaks of discipline, identity and pride. ZOOR exists to honour that — to give you the tools to wear your beard with confidence, every single day.

Natural Ingredients
