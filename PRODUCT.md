# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Grooming-conscious men, 25–45. They value quality and natural ingredients and are willing to pay a premium for products that match their self-image. They browse primarily on mobile. They are not impulsive buyers — they want to feel the brand before they buy.

## Product Purpose

ZOOR is a UK-based premium beard oil brand. The site exists to build enough brand conviction that a first-time visitor converts, and a returning customer feels at home. Success means the product page feels like a destination, not a checkout funnel.

## Brand Personality

Bold and expressive. A statement brand with strong opinions — not afraid to take up space. Strength in every drop. This is not a quiet, restrained luxury brand. It commands attention without shouting.

## Anti-references

- Generic Shopify stores: cookie-cutter dropshipping aesthetic, white background, endless product grid, zero personality. ZOOR should never look like it was assembled from a free theme.

## Design Principles

1. **Statement over safety** — every design choice should make a claim about the brand. Hedge toward personality, not toward generic.
2. **Earned premium** — the price point is justified through craft and intentional design, not through clichéd luxury signals (gold gradients, marble textures, "premium" typography).
3. **Show, don't list** — communicate product benefits through visual evidence and sensory cues, not bullet-point feature walls.
4. **Mobile-first confidence** — every hero moment must hit on a phone screen. The brand's boldness cannot rely on widescreen canvas.
5. **Conviction, not aspiration** — this brand knows who it is. Design choices should feel decided, not tentative.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard web accessibility. No formal WCAG level mandated. Ensure sufficient contrast given the dark theme and gold palette.
