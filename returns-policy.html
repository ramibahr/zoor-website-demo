Returns Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2024  |  Zoor Ltd  |  info@houseofzoor.co.uk

At ZOOR, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Returns Policy sets out your rights and our obligations in relation to returns, refunds and exchanges. Your statutory rights under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 and Consumer Rights Act 2015 are fully protected.

1. Your Right to Cancel (14-Day Cooling-Off Period)

Under the Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013, you have the right to cancel your order within 14 calendar days of receiving your goods — without giving any reason.

To exercise your right to cancel, you must notify us in writing before the 14-day period expires. You can do this by:

  • Emailing us at info@houseofzoor.co.uk with your order number and a clear statement that you wish to cancel

2. Conditions for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

  • The item must be returned within 14 days of notifying us of your wish to cancel
  • The item must be unused, in its original condition, and in its original packaging
  • For hygiene reasons, we are unable to accept returns of products that have been opened or used, unless the product is faulty or not as described

Items that do not meet these conditions will not be eligible for a refund, except where required by law.

3. How to Return an Item

  1. Contact us at info@houseofzoor.co.uk within 14 days of receiving your order to notify us of your intention to return
  2. We will respond within 2 business days with return instructions
  3. Carefully package the item and send it to the address provided in our response
  4. We recommend using a tracked postal service, as we cannot be responsible for items lost in transit

4. Return Postage Costs

Unless the item is faulty, damaged, or not as described, the cost of return postage is your responsibility. We are not liable for items lost or damaged in return transit.

If you are returning a faulty or incorrectly dispatched item, we will refund reasonable return postage costs upon receipt of proof of postage.

5. Refunds

5.1 Refund Amount

If your return is approved, we will refund the full price of the product, including the standard delivery charge you paid (if any). We are not obliged to refund any upgraded or express delivery surcharges.

5.2 Refund Processing Time

We will process your refund within 14 days of receiving the returned goods (or, if we have not received the goods, within 14 days of you providing evidence that you have returned them). Refunds will be made to the original payment method used at checkout.

5.3 Deductions

We reserve the right to make a deduction from your refund if the value of the goods has been reduced as a result of handling beyond what is necessary to establish the nature, characteristics and functioning of the goods.

6. Faulty or Damaged Items

If you receive a product that is faulty, damaged, or not as described, please contact us within 30 days of receipt at info@houseofzoor.co.uk with your order number and a description (and, if possible, photographs) of the issue.

Under the Consumer Rights Act 2015, you are entitled to:

  • A full refund within the first 30 days
  • A repair or replacement after 30 days (and a refund if repair/replacement is not possible)

7. Exchanges

We do not currently offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item, please return it for a refund and place a new order.

8. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened or used products (for hygiene reasons), unless faulty
  • Items returned after the 14-day return window without prior agreement
  • Items that have been damaged through misuse or normal wear and tear
